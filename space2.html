amazing space facts

Infinite iniverse

The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter and contains at least 2 trillion galaxies.

Time Dilation

Time moves slower near massive objects due to gravity's effect on spacetime, as predicted by Einstein's theory of relativity.

Neutron Stars

A sugar-cube-sized amount of neutron star material would weigh about a billion tons on Earth.

Space Sounds

While space is a vacuum and doesn't carry sound waves, NASA has converted electromagnetic vibrations into audible sounds.

Cosmic Speed

The Milky Way galaxy is moving through space at about 1.3 million miles per hour relative to the cosmic microwave background.

Water Worlds

There may be more water in Jupiter's moon Europa than all of Earth's oceans combined.
